2005
ConstróiAngola

 Best Participation Award

Read news translation

Fermat company distinguished as best participation in ConstróiAngola

Luanda, 28/11 •  Construction materials company Fermat was distinguished on Sunday, at the ConstroiAngola 2005 fair, with the prize for the best participation in the event, for having been the firm that presented the greatest innovation over the five days of the exhibition.

Fermat's marketing director, José Carlos Mourato, said after awarding the prize (a wooden sculpture of a thinker), that the distinction is the recognition of the work "of a team that gives it all to serve the Angolan market with the best there is in construction materials".

He referred that the event, which ended on Sunday, allowed his company to establish contacts and do some business, showing satisfaction with the results achieved by the company during the exhibition and hopes to participate in the next edition that will take place in 2006.

Alongside Fermat, other exhibitors were awarded by the organization of the third edition of the construction fair. the company MSTelecom (best participation in the telecommunications sector), Free Alarm (best participation in the security sector), service provider SGS (best participation in the services sector) and Odebrecht (best participation in the area of civil construction and public works).

Held at the premises of the Luanda International Fair (FILDA), the event took place this year in pavilion 6 (6,000 square meters) due to the coincidence of dates with the National Production Fair (FENAPRO).

The sample presented four exhibition halls which are ExpoConstrói, ExpoTelecom, InforAngola and, for the first time, ExpoSegur - for protection and security companies.

Read news translation

Model Karina Silva is honored today in Luanda

Luanda, 28/04 • Angolan model Karina Silva, second place in the Model of the World 2006 competition, will be honored today in Luanda. The tribute gala takes place at 10 pm at the São Jorge restaurant on the island of Luanda.

According to the Marketing Director of the construction materials company Fermat, institution that supports the event, José Carlos Mourato, the gala is promoted by Black Out Production, Frente Nova and São Jorge restaurant.

The 2006 edition of the Models of the World was held in March in the Tanzanian city Dar es Salaam and had the participation of more than 100 candidates.

The annual event aims to promote the image and socio-cultural values of the participating countries, as well as the discovery of new talents. This is the first edition of the Angolan model.

Born in Benguela, the 1.80 m tall mannequin began her career at the age of 14. And she is considered the most international Angolan model.

At national level, she has participated in all fashion events, from Moda Luanda, Angola Fashion Week and other shows in the capital and some provinces, having been distinguished three times with the award for best female model.

Internationally walked the catwalk in several countries, including France and Portugal.

Best Equipment Stand Award

Read news translation

IV edition of ConstróiAngola ends with positive balance

Luanda, 23/10 • The IV edition of the Civil Construction, Public Works and Security Fair (ConstóiAngola) held from the 18th to the 22nd of this month, in Luanda, produced an estimated turnover of USD 120 million, the previous edition which had a figure of USD 100 million.

During the five days, the 230 exhibitors from Angola, Portugal, Spain, China and Brazil presented products linked to civil construction and industrial safety and established an exchange between two companies (Tilca and H3P).

Despite the weak turnout of the public, the visit of the acting president of the National Assembly, João Lourenço, animated the exhibitors. The official appealed to businessmen to bet on local production to facilitate the acquisition of building materials.

From the range of companies, Odebrecht was distinguished with the prize for the best stand and participation, ICEP from Portugal as the best institutional participation, the company CIMACO in the field of construction companies, Fermat for the equipment on display, AP Service in the security area, Sonangol in the services sector and Tandek for innovation and technology.

Companies also took advantage of the event to present products or announce projects. Sonangol Distribuidora held a seminar in which it presented the advantages of using piped gas in homes and buildings, an existing service in the provinces of Luanda and Cabinda.

Odebrecht presented its project to build a shopping center in the southern zone of luanda, with 400 stores, restaurants, cinemas, bank and travel agencies, a parking lot and entertainment, among others.

Five companies win Christmas contest

Luanda, 19/01 • Five companies were considered winners of the 7th national public competition for the best store, commercial area and Christmas window, organized by the Ministry of Commerce, from December 10, 2006 to January 7 of the current year.

These are Mateba Hypermarket, Martal Supermarket, Imexo (furniture and decoration area), Fermat (construction materials), Milano (fashion and apparel) and Tatiana Pereira (Chamavo Shopping Center), in the respective categories.

In a note, the national directorate of internal trade refers that the classification obeyed, among other criteria, the decoration of spaces, exhibition of goods and services, originality, singularity and distinction, without, however, announcing the value of the prizes.

Organized within the scope of the festive season, the annual competition aimed to improve the techniques of showcase, decoration, as well as exhibition, with a view to better service to the public.

Read news translation

Organization rewards best participants

Luanda • The Constroi Angola 2010 Organization, awarded this Sunday night, on the occasion of the closing of the eighth edition, seven participants who stood out during the event.

Among the winners stand out the company Mota Engil in the category of "best participation for the most innovative stand", and the MCA Group, with the award of "best civil construction and public works company".

The Associação Industrial Portuguesa won the award for "best foreign participation" while Fermat was distinguished in the category of "best participation in construction materials".

The award for "best participation in civil construction services and public works" was given to the company CIMIANTO, while the "best participation in machines" was given to Emac.

With the project Terraços do Lobito, Odebrecht won the award for "Best Real Estate Project", in a ceremony that lasted almost an hour and whose relevant note was the announcement of the new name of ConstróiAngola.

The event, which will be known as PROJEKTA by ConstróiAngola from 2011 onwards, saw the presence of companies from Angola, South Africa, Portugal, Brazil, Germany, Egypt, China, Italy, Spain, United Arab Emirates and Korea.

Co-organized by FIL and ArenaDirect, ConstróiAngola took place under the motto "We build the future driven by a dream" and had the participation of 315 exhibitors, including national and international.
